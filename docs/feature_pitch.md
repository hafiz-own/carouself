# Future Features: Expanding the Vault

Our app currently has an amazing, distraction-free writing experience. But a centered editor can feel a bit "empty" when you aren't actively writing. To make the app feel alive, intelligent, and premium, here are some modern features we could build to fill out the experience:

## 1. The "Carouself" Memory Carousel (Core Identity Feature)
Leaning into the name **carouself**, we can build a literal, interactive 3D or smooth-sliding **Carousel of Memories**. 
- **Visual Browsing:** Instead of a simple vertical list in the sidebar, your Dashboard could feature a beautiful, swipeable carousel. Each "card" in the carousel represents a past entry, showing a snippet of text or an attached image.
- **"Spin to Reflect":** A shuffle button that physically spins the carousel and lands on a random entry from your past, encouraging you to reflect on memories you might have forgotten.
- **Time Machine:** The carousel can filter by "This time last month" or "One year ago today," making it a deeply personal nostalgia machine.

## 2. The "Home" Dashboard
Right now, if you don't have an entry selected, you just see an empty editor. We should replace this with a **Dashboard** that greets you.
- **Activity Graph**: A beautiful, GitHub-style contribution graph showing your writing streaks and consistency.
- **Quick Stats**: Total words written, total entries, and current daily streak.
- *This dashboard would beautifully house your Memory Carousel at the very top!*

## 3. Smart Prompts & Templates
Staring at a blank page is intimidating. We can add a subtle "Inspiration" section:
- **Daily Prompts**: An AI-curated or randomly selected question of the day (e.g., *"What is something you learned today that surprised you?"*).
- **Templates**: Quick buttons to generate a "Morning Gratitude" or "Evening Reflection" layout.

## 4. Mood & Emotion Tracking
Add a sleek, emoji-based mood selector at the top of the editor.
- You can tag an entry with a specific emotion.
- The Dashboard can then display a **Mood Heatmap**, allowing you to visualize your emotional trends over time alongside your writing habits.

## 5. Contextual Metadata (Auto-Tagging)
To make entries feel like true memories, we can automatically pull in contextual data when you create an entry:
- **Current Weather** (e.g., 🌧️ Rainy, 18°C)
- **Time of Day** tagging.
- Allow users to manually add custom tags (e.g., `#travel`, `#work`) that appear as pills below the title.
